Privacy Policy for ColorCrush

Effective Date: January 2025

Introduction

ColorCrush ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

ColorCrush is designed to protect your privacy. We do not directly collect, store, or process personal information. However, our app uses third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Data Storage

All game progress, including:

  • Level completion
  • Scores and stars
  • Lives and coins
  • Power-ups and purchases

is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

ColorCrush does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly.

Data Security

We do not transmit or store personal data. All game data remains on your device.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings → Privacy → Advertising
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with a new effective date.

Contact Us

ColorCrush Support

How to Play

ColorCrush is a match-3 puzzle game. Drag and swap adjacent blocks to match 3 or more of the same color. Reach the target score before running out of moves!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more lives?

Lives regenerate automatically every 20 minutes. You can also earn bonus lives by beating levels on your first try, or purchase them in the shop.

What are Streak Points?

Beat levels on your first try to earn streak points! Reach milestones (5, 10, 25 points) to get bonus lives and coins.

How do Power-Ups work?

Shuffle: Randomizes all blocks on the board
Extra Moves: Adds 5 moves to your current level
Purchase power-ups in the shop using coins.

How do I restore purchases?

Go to the Shop and look for "Restore Purchases" button. This will restore any previous in-app purchases.
